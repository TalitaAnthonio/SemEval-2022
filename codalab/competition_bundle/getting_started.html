How to Get Started

  • Step 1: Join our Google group here.
  • Step 2: Download the data on our task website.
  • Step 3: Have a look at the starter kit with baselines, a format checker and the evaluation metrics here.
  • Step 4: Register for the competition when the registration form is available.
  • Step 5: Submit your systems output predictions (see Evaluation page). The maximum number of submissions is 10.
